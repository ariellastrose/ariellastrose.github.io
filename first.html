Casino Style Salsa

Casino-Style is a type of dance that originated from Cuba. It became popular in the 1970s and is heavily influenced by styles of Cuban Son, Mambo, Cha Cha Cha, and Rumba Guaguanco. It differs from other styles because partners dance in a "rueda" or circle witha "catante" calling the moves.

LA-Style Salsa

LA-Style is danced on the first beat and in a line. It is strongly influenced by the Latin Hustle, Swing Argentine Tango, and Latin Ballroom Styles. There is a lot of emphasis placed on theatricality and acrobatics

Bachata

Bachata is a social dance that originates from the Dominican Republic. There are several different styles of bachata but we primarily teach Bachata Sensual. This style was created in Spain by Korke and Judith an mainly uses circular body movements, waves, isolations, and dips.
