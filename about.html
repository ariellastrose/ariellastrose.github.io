Ariella St. Rose

User Experience Designer, Dancer, Unicorn

Ariella St. Rose is an experience designer currently based in Miami.

She has worked on a multitude of projects, including those for How I Compare, Growers2Home, and AAA Productions. Her interest in interactive design began when she took her first course in interaction design at the University of Miami. She hopes to continue to gain more knowledge in the field and work on more projects

Ariella isn't only interested in design, she is completely obsessed with latin dance and is currently dancing with both Rico Mambo Dance Company and UFuerza. She has performed at multiple venues in the Miami area and trains under Joe Martin and Joanna Mancisidor Mateluna. She hopes to grow more as a dancer and spread her passion for latin dance with as many people as possible
